HEY THERE!

My name is Lauren Treat. I am a hair stylist and coloring enthusiast. I specialize in blonding, balayage, and creating natural-looking colors including gray blending and gray coverage. But more than anything, I enjoy helping my clients grow out and embrace their natural hair. Natural color, natural texture or curls, natural gray hairs—whatever makes you feel beautiful, you should OWN IT! I will always be your biggest cheerleader and help you find whatever makes you feel confident.

Getting married soon? Destination weddings, elopements, or right here in NWA, I'm available and excited to be a part of your big day. It always means so much to me when a bride asks me to be a part of her wedding.
